# Runday Business & Website Restructure — Design Spec

**Date:** 2026-06-01
**Status:** Approved
**Inspiration:** Custom AI Studio / Devin Karns playbook (value ladder, outcome-first positioning, named framework, selling relief)

---

## 1. Strategic Positioning

**From:** "AI automation agency for small businesses in Metro Manila"
**To:** "AI systems partner for business owners who'd rather grow than grind"

- Drop "automation agency" — positions as vendor. Use "systems partner."
- Drop "small businesses" and "Metro Manila" — stop capping ceiling.
- Tagline "We build the systems. AI runs them." moves to footer/secondary use.
- Named framework: **rundayOS** — elevated from animation label to core methodology.

### Target Market (Graduated Approach)

- **Now (0–6 months):** MSMBs in Metro Manila. Nail Oro Hotel. Get 3–5 more clients. Build case studies with real ROI numbers.
- **6–12 months:** Add metrics bar to site once real data exists. Start targeting multi-branch businesses (restaurant chains, clinic groups, hotel groups).
- **12–18 months:** Introduce AI Workshop as group offering. Push into mid-market.

## 2. Value Ladder (Free-to-Paid)

### Step 1 — Free Diagnostic Call
- 20-minute call. Listen, identify 1–2 biggest bottlenecks.
- CTA everywhere: "Book your free diagnostic" (replaces "Book a free call").
- "Diagnostic" sounds expert, not salesy.

### Step 2 — rundayOS Blueprint (starting at P15,000)
- Deep discovery: map current workflows, identify highest-ROI systems to build.
- Deliverable: a document they own — what to build, in what order, with projected time/money saved.
- ROI framing: "Most owners discover P20,000–P50,000/month in wasted time and missed revenue."
- Timeline: 1–2 weeks.

### Step 3 — rundayOS Build (starting at P30,000)
- Build and deploy the systems the blueprint identified.
- Modules available: Social & Content, Booking & Operations, Leads & Sales, Brand & Identity.
- ROI framing: "Clients typically save 15–25 hours/month within the first month."
- Timeline: 2–4 weeks per module.

### Step 4 — rundayOS Growth Partner (starting at P5,000/mo)
- Ongoing optimization, content creation, monitoring, monthly strategy calls.
- ROI framing: "Systems that keep compounding — not just running, but growing."
- Month-to-month. No lock-in.

---

## 3. Core Beliefs (replaces "Why Runday")

1. **ROI-driven** — Every system ties to a number: time saved, leads captured, revenue gained. Not experiments.
2. **No new tools to learn** — We build on Messenger, Google, Canva, Notion — things you already use every day.
3. **Yours forever** — Every account, every asset, every login. If we part ways tomorrow, everything keeps running.

## 5. Services Page Restructure (services.html)

### Primary structure: 4 phases (value ladder)

Each phase gets a full section with description, what's included, timeline, and pricing with ROI framing.

### Build phase modules (nested under Build):

Presented as selectable modules the blueprint might recommend:

- **Social & Content** — AI-generated posts, scheduling, ad campaigns, review generation, SEO.
- **Booking & Operations** — Booking flows, dashboards, SMS reminders, email automations, Notion systems.
- **Leads & Sales** — Auto-replies, lead capture, CRM setup, Messenger bots, follow-up sequences.
- **Brand & Identity** — Logo, design systems, Canva templates, brand guidelines.

### Pricing display:

- Blueprint: "Starting at P15,000 — most owners uncover P20K–P50K/mo in hidden inefficiency"
- Build: "Starting at P30,000 — clients typically reclaim 15–25 hours/month"
- Growth Partner: "Starting at P5,000/mo — your systems keep compounding"

No more Starter/Growth/Full Suite tiers. No more Maintain/Maintain+Manage retainers. Everything flows through the value ladder.
