how the math works

No tricks. Just the numbers.

We take your daily inquiries across a 30-day month, keep the share that currently go unanswered or arrive after hours, then apply a conservative booking rate to estimate how many of those you could have won with an instant, always-on reply. Multiply by your average booking value and you get the revenue walking out the door. The hours figure assumes each handled inquiry takes about three minutes of someone's time. These are estimates to start a conversation, not a forecast. On a free diagnostic we replace the assumptions with your real numbers.
