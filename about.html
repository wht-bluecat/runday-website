who we are

One founder.
One mission.

Philippine small businesses are drowning in manual work that AI can handle. Runday exists to fix that — together.

Runday started with a simple observation: most small businesses in Manila are doing the same repetitive work every day — posting on social media, chasing leads, sending reminders, managing bookings. All of it can be automated.

We're not a traditional agency. We don't sell you retainers and monthly reports. We build AI-powered systems that do the actual work — then hand them over. You own every account, every automation, every template.

Think of us as your systems partner. You tell us what's taking up your time. We'll figure out the right system together — and build it for you.
