who we are

The back office you
can't afford to hire.

Small business owners in Manila are buried in work that should run itself, answering messages, chasing reviews, following up on leads, posting content. Runday builds the back office that handles it. Run by AI, watched by a human.

Runday started with a simple observation: most small businesses in Manila are doing the same repetitive work every day, posting on social media, chasing leads, sending reminders, managing bookings. It's the work a back office would handle, if they could afford one. So we built one that runs on AI.

We're not an agency. We're your back office. Agencies hand you deliverables and disappear. We diagnose where your business is leaking time and money, design the right system, build it, and then run it with AI on an ongoing basis, so the work keeps getting done without you and the results compound instead of stall.

You own every account, every asset, every login. What you get from Growth Partner isn't lock-in, it's the relief of not having to run the systems yourself. We do that. You run your business.
