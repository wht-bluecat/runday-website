who we are

We build the systems.
AI runs them.

Small business owners in Manila are buried in work that should run itself. Runday diagnoses what's broken, designs the fix, builds the systems, and keeps them running — so you don't have to.

Runday started with a simple observation: most small businesses in Manila are doing the same repetitive work every day — posting on social media, chasing leads, sending reminders, managing bookings. All of it can be systemised — and then run, permanently, with AI.

We're not a traditional agency. We don't build things and disappear. We diagnose where your business is leaking time and money, design the right system, build it, and then operate it with AI on an ongoing basis — so the results compound instead of stall.

You own every account, every asset, every login. What you get from Growth Partner isn't lock-in — it's the relief of not having to run the systems yourself. We do that. You run your business.
