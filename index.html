questions

The things everyone asks.

What's a diagnostic call?

A free 20-minute conversation where we listen to what's not working in your business and identify the biggest bottleneck. No pitch, no commitment — just clarity on what to fix first.

What's a rundayOS Blueprint?

A deep dive into your operations. We map your workflows, identify the highest-leverage systems to build, and deliver a document with ROI projections. It's yours to keep even if you don't hire us to build.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. We build on tools you already use — Messenger, Google, Canva, Notion. If you can post on Facebook, you can run what we build.

Who owns the systems?

You do — every account, every asset, every login. No lock-in. If we part ways tomorrow, everything keeps running.

How long does it take?

Blueprint: 1–2 weeks. Build: 2–4 weeks per module. Most businesses are up and running within a month.

What does it cost?

Blueprint starts at ₱15,000. Build starts at ₱30,000. Growth Partner starts at ₱5,000/mo. The diagnostic call is free. Final scope always comes from the blueprint — no surprises. See full pricing →
